About Us

Welcome to the exciting world of clogging! We are a vibrant and passionate clogging dance group based in the beautiful regions of Southeast Michigan and Northwest Ohio. With our roots deeply intertwined in the rich cultural heritage of American tap dancing and Irish step dancing, we bring to life the rhythmic magic of clogging.

At our core, we are a performance-oriented ensemble, showcasing our lively and energetic dance routines at a wide range of events and venues. From the thrill of fairs and festivals to the heartwarming visits to nursing homes, our cloggers spread joy and entertainment wherever they go. We add flair to reunions, fundraisers, and birthday parties, making every occasion a memorable one with our toe-tapping rhythms and synchronized movements.

Unlike some clogging groups that focus on competition, our philosophy centers on fostering a supportive and inclusive environment for all dancers. We are a non-competitive group, prioritizing the enjoyment of learning and dancing over winning trophies. With a belief in the power of encouragement, we motivate each other to do our best, celebrate individual progress, and embrace the camaraderie that comes with being part of our dance family.

Clogging, with its fascinating history, holds a special place in the realm of dance. Originating on the porches of Appalachian Mountain communities, this lively form of expression has evolved over time, incorporating unique elements like the distinctive buck taps on our shoes. Our dedicated instructors teach a repertoire of routines that encompass the eight basic clogging steps, allowing us to create captivating performances set to a diverse range of music genres, from classic country melodies to the latest pop hits.
